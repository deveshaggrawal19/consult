HUMAN RESURCES JOBS

Human resource assistants are the behind-the-scenes collaborators and colleagues whose main job responsibilities are focused on helping HR directors and managers accomplish HR-related tasks. A good human resources employee develops and manages their company’s culture. They recruit new hires, maintain benefits and payroll, mediate conflict and engage in training and development. Their role is at the core of a company’s success. Entering the field with a bachelor’s degree gives you access to more options, including working in employee relations, HR information systems, training and development, labor relations or as a compensation or benefit analyst. There are lots of opportunities in this field and many giant companoes are looking for good candidates for this role.
Job Responsibilities


  • Compile and update employee records.
  • Plan, execute, and measure experiments and conversion tests
  • Process documentation and prepare reports relating to personnel activities (staffing, recruitment, training, grievances, performance evaluations etc)
  • Compiling projects like meetings, training etc and reporting
  • Clearing doubt and Employee question. Creating and distributing documents Providing customer service to organization employees
  • Assist with day to day operations of the HR functions and duties
  • Setting appointments and arranging meetings,
  • Provide clerical and administrative support to Human Resources executives
  • Deal with employee requests regarding human resources issues, rules, and regulations
  • Coordinate communication with candidates and schedule and conduct interviews
  • Conduct initial orientation to newly hired employees
