ACCOUNTING JOBS

A qualified Accountant has to handle prepare, compute, manage, research and analyze all accounting data, in order to provide quantitative information on performance, financial position, solvency, liquidity and cash flows of our business. While hiring for accountant, companies required having working experience as a cost accountant or in a relevant field and also have knowledge of accounting and corporate finance principles and procedures. an accountant, will spend their time filing bills from vendors or entering the same kind of transaction over and over. There are so many vacancies of accountants in companies.
Job Responsibilities


  • Prepares asset, liability, and capital account entries by compiling and analyzing account information.
  • Preparing financial and other statements.
  • Prepares payments by verifying documentation, and requesting disbursements.
  • Documents financial transactions by entering account information.
  • Recommends financial actions by analyzing accounting options.
  • Summarizes current financial status by collecting information; preparing balance sheet, profit and loss statement, and other reports.
  • Manage all accounting operations based on accounting principles
  • Substantiates financial transactions by auditing documents.
  • Keep information confidential and secure them with random database backups
  • Keep up with financial policies, regulation and legislation
  • Issue invoices and update accounts receivable, payable and perform reconciliations
  • Answers accounting procedure questions by researching and interpreting accounting policy and regulations.
  • Provide internal and external auditing services.
