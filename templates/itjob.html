IT JOBS

Over the years, Indian IT companies feverishly built their so-called pyramid model that involved hiring thousands of fresh engineering graduates annually and quickly deploying them on software projects to ensure overall costs remained low. This industry creates lakhs of IT jobs in india and employs around 4 million people. IT sector is one of the most booming sectors in India as majority of white-collar jobs are focused in this sector. The sector has increased its contribution to India's GDP from 1.2% in 1998 to 7.5% in 2012. IT sector is primarily divided in 2 domains i.e. Product-based companies and Service-based companies. Product based companies are those that create some products like Microsoft, PayTM, Amazon, etc are product based companies. Service-based companies are those that work on these products to provide service to the client. For Example: TCS, Wipro etc are service-based companies.
